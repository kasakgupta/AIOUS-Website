SPONSORSHIP

We, the team of AIOUS, are excited to invite you to partner with us and put your company in the spotlight at Formula Bharat and Supra SAE. These prestigious national events attract over 2500 engineering students from universities across the country, as well as numerous motorsport enthusiasts. By participating, your brand will gain tremendous visibility and demonstrate a strong commitment to nurturing the talent and potential of budding engineers.

Our mission is to empower women in engineering, and we believe your involvement can make a significant impact. By aligning with us, you will not only promote your products among the participating teams but also showcase your dedication to gender diversity in engineering. Your support will help us highlight our capabilities and foster an environment where women excel.

Moreover, we are dedicated to supporting you in promoting any recently launched products. We will ensure that your brand and products receive extensive publicity at the events and continue to be highlighted within and beyond our college even after the event concludes.

Join us in this incredible journey to empower the next generation of engineers and gain unparalleled publicity opportunities. Let's work together to create a lasting impact and demonstrate our shared commitment to innovation and gender diversity in engineering.
