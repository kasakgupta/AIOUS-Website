About Us

AIOUS Formula Student comprises passionate Mechanical and Automation Engineering students dedicated to conceptualizing, designing, and fabricating an exemplary car showcasing advanced engineering techniques. Our commitment lies in sincere teamwork and excellence, recognizing the paramount importance of collaboration and competence in achieving our goals.
We, the students of IGDTUW, are honored to represent AIOUS in the esteemed Formula Bharat Competition 2025. The legacy of our team, established by our predecessors in 2011, serves as a guiding light for us.
